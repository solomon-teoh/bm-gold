BM GOLD FARM

Was established in 2017 after Solomon Teoh discovered grafting. 5 years ago, his friend, Daniel Ong, switched this 6.7-acre durian farm to pesticide-free organic farming to produce healthy, high-quality durian. Daniel and his business friend, Loo Yu Zhe, noticed that their durian became more flavorful, and have identified 5 flavors including a unique “numb” taste that they want to share with you now. Daniel ONG and his business friend discovered the numb taste by accident one day picking durian at dawn. “POP!!!” A durian dropped suddenly nearby. The smell was so strong that Daniel Ong couldn’t help but say, “Wow!” So they quickly searched for that durian with their torch light. Five minutes later, they found the durian on the ground beside a huge rock. The durian had hit the rock and split open into a mess. “It is a waste,” said Loo Yu Zhe, and he decided that since they couldn’t sell the durian he should eat it. So he ate it right there on the rock. “Oh My God!” he exclaimed. It made his mouth feel totally cool and fizzy, like a sweet and bitter breath mint. daniel Ong then tasted it and was shocked.
